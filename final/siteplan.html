Site Plan

Site Name: Wicketpedia

This name is a clever combination of the site name Wikipedia and a famous Ewok named Wicket from Star Wars

Purpose

The purpose of the site will be to inform the audience about Star Wars and provide background on the movies and more information

Scenarios

  • What is a summary of the Original Star Wars Trilogy
  • Who were the most powerful Jedi in the movies?
  • Where can I go for more information about Star Wars
