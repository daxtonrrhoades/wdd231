Star Wars Summary

Original Trilogy

A farmboy named Luke Skywalker is sent by Old Ben, also known as Obi Wan Kenobi, to rescue princess Leia who has been taken hostage by Darth Vader.

He is accompanied by the Captain of the Millenium Falcon, Han Solo, and his fuzzy friend Chewbacca along with Old Ben and the droids, R2D2 and C3PO.

On the Death Star Obi Wan Sacrifices himself to get the others to their ship safely and stays behind with Vader and falls at his hand.

In the next film, we start on the snowy planet Hoth and Luke and his friends are attacked by Imperial forces commanded by Darth Vader.

Luke travels to Dagobah to learn the ways of the Jedi and meets Yoda, an old friend of Ben and a Jedi Master who will teach Luke.

Luke is influenced by the force to go help his friends and there he learns that Darth Vader is his father. Han is also frozen in Carbonite.

In the next film Luke is determined to bring his father back to the light side. He returns to Dagobah to finish his training and Yoda passes away.

Han is freed from Carbonite at Jabba's Palace and Luke is thrown into the Rancor's pit to be eaten up. Luke is victorious and they escape the palace.

Luke is put up against Vader and his master the Emperor as Luke's friends plan to blow up the second Death Star

Luke convinces Vader there is still good in him and together they defeat Sidious. Though due to being electricuted Vader does not survive

Weapons and Vehicles

E-11 Blaster

E-11

Description

The E-11 is one of the most common blasters used by Imperial Stormtroopers in the Original Trilogy of Star Wars.

A basic imperial automatic blaster rifle

Lightsaber

Lightsaber

Description

The Lightsaber is one of the most well known weapons of Star Wars. It is a blade made of light that expands and retracts from a small hilt.

They come in multiple colors depending on the Kyber crystal enclosed inside.

The most known users are Luke Skywalker, Obi Wan Kenobi, and Darth Vader

X-Wing Starfighter

X-Wing

Description

The X-Wing is one of the most well known starfighters of the Rebellion in the Original Trilogy of Star Wars.

It is most well known because Luke Skywalker flies an X-wing in the Original Trilogy and crashes it in a Dagobah swamp.

Tie Fighter Starfighter

Tie Fighter

Description

The Tie Fighter is one of the most well known starfighters of the Empire in the Original Trilogy of Star Wars.

It is used by the empire in star fighter dogfights throughout the trilogy.

Death Star Space Station

Death Star

Description

The Death Star was a weaponized galactic space station used by the empire and Darth Vader and The Emperor themselves.

It was used to blow up planets and was a main operating base for the empire.

Alderaan was the first planet to be blown up by the first Death Star.

The Death Star had to be rebuilt after the first one was blown up by the Rebellion.

74-Z Speeder Bike

74-Z Speeder

Description

74-Z Speeder Bike was a high speed hover bike used on certain planets by the empire.

It appears on the planet Endor in "Return of the Jedi" being ridden by imperial scout troopers

Luke and Leia ride these in a high speed chase after scout troopers on Endor
